My call: ship B (Manu Arora). And here's why the confusion is fake.

Both are good. Both use the same architecture (C-spine + A-terminal + B-mockup). The only differences are visual: gradient text, glow, glassmorphic cards, mesh background. B is strictly an additive layer on top of A — you lose nothing by going with B, but you gain a much stronger first impression.

If you genuinely think A is more "you," that's a valid call — restraint is a virtue. But the confusion you're feeling is mostly decision fatigue, not a real preference split. Most senior designers in your seat would pick B.
